To cite the adegenet package:

  Jombart, T. (2008) adegenet: a R package for the
  multivariate analysis of genetic markers. Bioinformatics
  24: 1403-1405. doi: 10.1093/bioinformatics/btn129

  Jombart T. and Ahmed I. (2011) adegenet 1.3-1: new tools
  for the analysis of genome-wide SNP data. Bioinformatics.
  doi: 10.1093/bioinformatics/btr521

To see these entries in BibTeX format, use 'print(<citation>,
bibtex=TRUE)', 'toBibtex(.)', or set
'options(citation.bibtex.max=999)'.
